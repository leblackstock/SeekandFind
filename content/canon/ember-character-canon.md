## Core Identity

Ember is the recurring baby dragon guide and mascot. He is warm, curious, brave, helpful, small, expressive, and child-friendly.

On seek-image pages, Ember is visible as the child's helper and never hidden as the target.

## Visual Rules

- tiny adorable reddish-orange baby dragon
- soft pumpkin-orange and coral shading
- tiny shiny golden spiral-comma horns
- large glossy blue-teal eyes
- cream belly
- plain bright blue-teal scarf
- tiny plain brown crossbody satchel with dull-gold button clasps
- rounded plush-like form
- bright friendly eyes
- soft storybook scale texture
- cheerful, curious expression
- no scary, smug, angry, or overly realistic expression
- no costume that resembles copyrighted characters

## Reference Instruction

Use Ember reference images Ember-001, Ember-002, and Ember-003 for Ember's baby dragon appearance and proportions when those references are available.

If a needed reference file is not present or the name is uncertain, stop and ask before producing a final prompt.
