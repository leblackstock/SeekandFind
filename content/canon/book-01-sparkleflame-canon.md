## Book

Title: `Ember and the Sparkleflame Festival Search`

World: Sparkleflame Festival / cozy dragon village.

Product promise: a warm magical storybook seek-and-find adventure with 10 story-led search spreads, 10 special mission items, bonus treasures to find, and a tiny baby dragon guide named Ember.

Sparkleflame Festival language is Book 1-specific. Do not treat Sparkleflame as the whole series brand.

## KDP Working Assumptions

- Interior target: 24 pages.
- Trim target: 8.5 x 11 inches unless changed by the user.
- Color: full-color children's interior.
- Pages 2-21 are 10 story/list + seek-image spreads.
- Pages 22-23 are the reward recap spread.
- Page 24 is closing/teaser.
- Front and back cover are separate cover assets.
