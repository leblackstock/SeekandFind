## Core Identity

Ember is the recurring baby dragon guide and mascot. He is warm, curious, brave, helpful, small, expressive, and child-friendly.

On seek-image pages, Ember is visible as the child's helper and never hidden as the target.

## Visual Rules

- tiny adorable reddish-orange baby dragon
- soft pumpkin-orange and coral shading
- tiny shiny golden spiral-comma horns
- large glossy blue-teal eyes
- cream belly
- plain bright blue-teal scarf
- tiny plain brown crossbody satchel with dull-gold button clasps
- rounded plush-like form
- bright friendly eyes
- soft storybook scale texture
- cheerful, curious expression
- no scary, smug, angry, or overly realistic expression
- no costume that resembles copyrighted characters

# Visual Style Guide

Source mirror: active Ember prompt and marketing rules.

Use polished soft 2.25D children's storybook illustration style with lightly illustrated edges, rounded plush-like forms, gentle sculpted volume, painterly shading, warm cheerful lighting, smooth clean surfaces, and magical but readable staging.

Do not use flat cartoon styling, photorealism, overly 3D rendering, scary lighting, muddy clutter, or protected brand imitation.

For seek pages, build clear foreground, midground, and background zones. Place objects where they naturally belong. Keep paths, bridges, door thresholds, and the viewer's eye path mostly open and readable.

For marketing art, keep the image clear at phone size and make sure it does not pretend to be a real interior page, real cover, listing preview, or sample-page asset unless real book assets are used.


# No Readable Text Policy

Source mirror: active Ember rules and marketing prompt pack.

## Interior Image Prompts

Do not request readable generated text inside AI-generated seek-page images. No labels, signs, page numbers, watermarks, fake logos, gibberish text, decorative unreadable writing, or accidental lettering.

Story/list pages, cover typography, listing graphics, and marketing posts should use real editable layout text unless the user explicitly approves exact text generation for a marketing image.

## Marketing Image Prompts

For every marketing prompt:

- If text should appear, list the exact words to render.
- If text should not appear, say `No text.` clearly.
- Do not allow extra words, fake text, gibberish text, misspellings, labels, watermarks, page numbers, or decorative unreadable writing.

## Hidden Object Marking

Never mark the mission item or hidden objects with a circle, square, box, outline, arrow, halo, label, highlight, or answer mark on seek-page art. Markers are allowed only in separate answer-key or hint assets created after final art approval.


## 15-Second Structure

Beat 1, 0:00-0:03: Establish a warm Ember world moment tied to promote Ember seek-and-find book.
Beat 2, 0:03-0:07: Ember moves gently through the scene: Ember waves, notices a glowing festival clue, follows sparkles, and smiles at the viewer.
Beat 3, 0:07-0:11: A glowing clue or search moment gives the clip visual payoff.
Beat 4, 0:11-0:15: Ember smiles toward the viewer with a cozy invitation to search.

## Image-To-Video Prompt

Use Ember reference images Ember-001, Ember-002, and Ember-003 for character consistency. Keep Ember's scarf, satchel, horns, eyes, and reddish-orange baby-dragon proportions on-model.

Animate a soft 2.25D children's storybook scene. Use gentle camera motion: slow push-in, slight parallax, and tiny sparkle movement. Keep motion readable and calm for children ages 5-8.

## Camera Movement

- Slow push-in toward Ember.
- Gentle parallax between foreground decorations and background festival/world details.
- No frantic cuts, scary lighting, or dizzying motion.
