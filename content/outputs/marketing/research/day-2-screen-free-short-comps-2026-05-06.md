## Takeaways For Ember Day 2

- Use short exact text, not paragraph captions.
- Give the viewer one clear action: pause, look, save.
- Keep music soft, bright, and child-friendly.
- Do not make the character appear to speak unless the mouth animation is controllable.
- If generated text is unreliable, add captions and sound in CapCut as editable elements after creating the motion clip.
