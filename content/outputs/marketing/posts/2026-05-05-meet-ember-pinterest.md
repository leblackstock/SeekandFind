- Do not use TypeScript-only syntax such as `el as HTMLTextAreaElement` inside inline `npx tsx -` browser snippets. The inline runner treated it as JavaScript and failed before interacting with the page.
- Do not fill the last textarea blindly on Pinterest Pin Builder. Pinterest includes a hidden `g-recaptcha-response` textarea; target the visible alt-text textarea by its placeholder: `Explain what people can see in the Pin`.
- After clicking `Add alt text`, re-inspect visible textarea fields before filling. The correct field is the visible textarea for screen-reader text, not the hidden reCAPTCHA response.
