## Character Use

Ember is the recurring guide on every seek page. Use Ember reference images `Ember-001`, `Ember-002`, and `Ember-003`.

Book 4 named supporting characters must come from canon only:

- Pebblekins may appear on tide-pool, stone, driftwood, and lighthouse-step pages. Use `Pebblekins-001`, `Pebblekins-002`, and `Pebblekins-003`.
- Hootiepuff may appear on moonlit clue-spotting pages. Use `HootiePuff-001`, `HootiePuff-002`, and `HootiePuff-003`.
- Luma Leafwhisk may appear on kelp, garden, and reef pages. Use `Luma_Leafwhisk-001`, `Luma_Leafwhisk-002`, and `Luma_Leafwhisk-003`.
- Elder Glowkeeper may appear on the lighthouse/finale guidance pages. Use `Elder_Glowkeeper-001`, `Elder_Glowkeeper-002`, and `Elder_Glowkeeper-003`.

Do not introduce new named side characters in Book 4.
