Printed order is story/list left page then seek image right page. Production order is seek image first, then story/list page after the find list is approved:

## Character Use

Ember is the recurring guide on every seek page. Use Ember reference images `Ember-001`, `Ember-002`, and `Ember-003`.

Book 6 named supporting characters must come from canon only: Luma Leafwhisk for garden pages, Hootiepuff for clue-spotting pages, Pebblekins for stone-path pages, Gemma Glint for one rainbow-gem greenhouse page, and Elder Glowkeeper for the finale. Do not introduce new named side characters.

## Pages 22-23 Recap / Reward Spread

Show Ember proudly celebrating the 10 recovered mission items:

Rainbow Gate Key; Petal Glow Compass; Bumbleberry Tart; Speckled Seed Clue; Painted Pebble Token; Dragonfly Ribbon; Rainbow Gem Sprout; Storyflower Scroll; Sunbeam Lens; Rainbow Crown Lantern.

Display the 10 recovered mission items on separate small Rainbow Garden pedestals, like a cheerful reward exhibit. Use petal-shaped stands, painted pebble bases, vine curls, rainbow garden colors, tiny flower accents, and soft sunbeam glow. Split the display into five pedestals on the left page and five pedestals on the right page. Keep every pedestal and mission item fully outside the center gutter/crease safety lane. Ember may celebrate from one page, but keep Ember's face, eyes, horns, scarf knot, satchel, tail, and main body entirely on one printed page and away from the crease.

Do not show answer locations. This is a reward spread, not an answer key.
